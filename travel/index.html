How to get there

Arriving by car

If you arrive by car, both from the North and from the South, take the Valdichiana exit from the A1 highway, which is called Autostrada del Sole. Right after the toll gate exit take the E45 freeway (slip road from Siena to Perugia) in the direction to Perugia and then take the second exit for Cortona (Cortona San Lorenzo). You will just need to follow the street signs to arrive to the SS71 road in Camucia and to continue to Cortona. Car parks are provided really close to the hackathon venue, for free.

TIEMME bus service

If you arrive by train to Camucia, Terontola, Arezzo or Castiglion Fiorentino train stations, you can go to Cortona taking advantage of "La Ferroviaria Italiana Spa Arezzo" bus service. For up- to-date information about the timetable and the ticket prices, look up the company website www.lfi.it.

Arriving by plane

Florence Peretola International Airport (about 120 Km away) - www.aeroporto.firenze.it
Perugia Sant’Egidio International Airport (about 60 Km away - 50 minutes by car) - www.airport.umbria.it
Pisa International Airport (about 220 Km away - 2 hours by car) - www.pisa-airport.com Roma Fiumicino International Airport (about 230 Km away - 2 and a half hours by car) - www.adr.it

Arriving by train

Florence-Rome railway line, Terontola or Camucia train stations Terontola-Perugia-Assisi-Terni railway line, Terontola train station Once arrived at these train stations, you can easily go to Cortona by taxi or by bus. For further information about the timetable and the ticket prices, look up the National Railway Company “Ferrovie dello Stato” website - www.ferroviedellostato.it
